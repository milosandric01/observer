How it works

Features

See every page a visitor viewed, what they clicked, and how far they scrolled, so you know exactly where they got stuck or gave up.

Rage clicks, dead clicks and JavaScript errors are flagged automatically, so the moments that quietly kill conversions surface on their own.

Privacy-first by default

No cookies, GDPR & CCPA ready, and all sensitive input is masked before it ever leaves the visitor's browser.
