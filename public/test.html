Frequently asked questions

Do I need to install anything?

Nope. Just paste a single script tag and you're live in 30 seconds.

Is it GDPR compliant?

Yes. We don't use cookies and never collect personal data.

Can I track single-page apps?

Absolutely. We detect SPA navigation automatically via the History API.

How does section tracking work?

Add observer-section="name" to any element. When 50% of it enters the viewport for at least 1 second, we fire a section_view event.
